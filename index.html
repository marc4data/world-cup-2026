Storylines · projections at a glance

Group of Death — Group I
France (model #2, 13% title) drawn with Haaland's Norway and Senegal — the only group with two heavyweights. France–Norway is a Matchday-3 knockout.
Softest draws — Spain & Argentina
Spain (Group H) holds the field's best QF odds (52%); Argentina (Group J) drew Austria/Algeria/Jordan (45% QF). Both top seeds dodged a strong #2.
Dark horse — Morocco (Group C)
2022 semi-finalists carry the highest QF odds of any non-traditional power (19%) — but share Group C with Brazil.
Most wide-open — Group D
USA, Türkiye, Paraguay, Australia: no team above ~1% to win it, and host USA only 77% to advance. A true toss-up (Group B close behind).
The hosts
All three favored to reach the knockouts — Mexico 87%, Canada 80%, USA 77% — but none is a title threat (each ≤1.2%).
